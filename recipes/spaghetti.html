How to: Spaghetti

Here is a simple Spaghetti recipe. It is very simple to make, and takes very little to no time.

This low calorie Spaghetti packs a punch of flavor and are great for meal prep.

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti Pasta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Virgin Olive Oil
  • Fresh Garlic
  • Oregan
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Cooking Steps:

  1. Cook spaghetti: Fill a large Dutch oven or pot with cold water 3/4 full and bring to a boil. Add 2 tsp salt and spaghetti.
  2. Ensure pasta does not stick together: Separate pasta with tongs a few times during the first 2 minutes of cooking to ensure it does not stick. Keep cooking uncovered for 5 more minutes or until al dente, stirring occasionally.
  3. Drain pasta: Test the spaghetti for doneness towards the end by grabbing one strand onto a plate with tongs. Do not overcook until too soft. The key to tasty pasta is cooked pasta with a bite. Drain in a colander.
  4. Cook garlic: Return the pot to medium heat and add the olive oil, 4 garlic cloves, and oregano. Cook for 20 seconds, stirring constantly. Garlic cooks fast and can easily burn, so don’t walk away. Turn off the heat.
  5. Add remaining ingredients: Drained pasta, tomato sauce, remaining 1/2 tsp salt, ground black pepper, and 1 more fresh garlic clove. Stir gently until warmed through. That 1 clove of fresh garlic at the end is a must!
  6. Sprinkle with Parmesan…and serve this spaghetti with tomato sauce immediately!
  7. Enjoy!
